Your Virtual Kitchen

We know how frustrating it is to stay home all day, but we're here to make home and kitchen fun again! Join us for a virtual cooking experience, where you can hang out with friends and family and cook together remotely.

Here's how it works:


1. Think of somebody that you would like to cook and eat with remotely.

2. Fill out a quick survey to cater your experience and schedule your hangout.

3. We will follow up with your plan - what you’ll be cooking, what ingredients, your need, and instructions for how to join the call.

4. Enjoy your experience! Bitesize will guide you through the process via a remote zoom call .
